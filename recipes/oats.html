Oats

Description:

Simple and tasty breakfast. Serves 1. To be prepared in the microwave.

Ingredients:

  • 3 tablespoons of oats
  • Cinnamon powder
  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Fruit
  • Salt

Method:

  1. Soak the oats in approximately 50ml of water and a pinch of salt for 3 minutes.
  2. Microwave the oats in an appropriate container for about 3 minutes.
  3. Stir the oats every 30 seconds or so to avoid oats spilling out of the container.
  4. Cut up some fruit of your choice.
  5. Add the fruit and a pinch of cinnamon to your oats.
  6. Add the butter to the oats and enjoy.
