Potato and boiled egg mayo salad

Description:

A BBQ and Sunday lunch favourite. This potato salad recipe needs minimal ingredients and serves 4. To be prepared on stovetop.

Ingredients:

  • 3 large potatoes
  • 2 large eggs
  • A small red onion
  • Parsley
  • Mayonnaise

Method:

  1. Rinse the potatoes, place them in a pot with boiling water and a pinch of salt. Boil the potatoes for 10 to 15 minutes.
  2. Boil eggs for 6 minutes or until hard boiled.
  3. Allow both potatoes and eggs to cool down.
  4. Slice the potatoes into cubes and cut up the boiled eggs, add both into one bowl.
  5. Chop the red onion into fine cubes and add to the potatoes and eggs.
  6. Add a few tablespoons of mayonnaise and gently stir the mixture.
  7. Garnish with some parsely and store in the fridge until ready to serve.
