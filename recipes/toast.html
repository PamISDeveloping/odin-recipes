Toasted cheese sandwich.

Description:

Toast sandwich with cheese. A lunchbox favourite.Serves 1.

Ingredients:

  • Four slices of your preferred bread
  • Butter
  • Cooking oil
  • Non-stick spray
  • Salt and pepper
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Tomato
  • 2 eggs

Method:

  1. Add a tablespoon of cooking oil to a pan and fry the 2 eggs on a stovetop.
  2. Add salt and pepper to the fried eggs and place them aside.
  3. Cut up a tomato into slices.
  4. Spread all 4 slices of bread with butter.
  5. Grate about 300g of cheddar cheese and add to 2 slices of the bread.
  6. Add the fried egg and tomato onto the cheese and finally add the other 2 slices on top.
  7. Toast the sandwich in a triangle toaster.Make sure the toaster is first sprayed with a non-stick spray.
